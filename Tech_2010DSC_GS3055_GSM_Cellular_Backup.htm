DSC GS3055-I GSM Cellular Backup

Information Similar to TelGuard TG-4, uses dialer capture to transfer signal only if phone lines are down

Compatible with any panel that supports Contact ID

Can decode 10-digit Contact ID however will only send 4-digit Contact ID to central station

Sends directly by cellular to monitoring station via GPRS data channel across Internet to DSC Internet receiver

We cannot at this time monitor these

Power 9 to 14VDC

120mA standby

500mA transmitting

Must use included 700mA power supply or a panel that can supply up to 500mA current

(in addition to supplying power to keypads, motions, smoke detectors etc)

LP3 Jumper OFF = 120mA current limited, must use backup battery (default)

ON = 70mA power required, do not connect backup battery

LED Status (Units that are not yet registered will flash top Red and Yellow LED's)

Triangle (Red) 1 Flash = Backup Battery absent or low

2 Flashes = Radio/SIM failure (Try reseating SIM card)

3 Flashes = GSM network problem (low signal strength, SIM card not activated)

4 Flashes = Low signal strength

5 Flashes = Connect 24 configuration SMS failure (improper VRU programming)

6 Flashes = Central station Receiver not available (improper VRU programming, no receiver)

7 Flashes = Power Supply Trouble (DC power absent, running on battery)

Phone (Yellow) ON = On GSM network, land-line absent (1 blink = GPRS transmit, 2 blinks = GPRS receive)

OFF = Phone line is active, GS3055-I is not active

Signal Strength Both ON = Optimal

(Bottom Green LED's) Bottom ON = Good

Bottom Flashing = Poor

Both OFF = No Signal/No GSM network available

Zone Triggers Default values

ZN1 = Fire

ZN2 = Audible Panic

ZN3 = Burg

ZN4 = System Trouble

Registration SIM card activation can take up to 24 hours

Activation is done on Connect 24 website at www.connect24.com or call DSC VRU at 866-910-3865

  1. Press 6 for activation

  2. Enter Installer ID issued to dealer by Connect 24

  3. Enter 4-digit Installer PIN number issued to dealer by Connect 24

  4. Enter 19 or 20-digit SIM card number from sticker under GS3055-I

  1. Enter 0 for backup or 1 for primary communicator

  2. Enter 0 for No test transmissions, 1 for daily, 2 for weekly or 3 for monthly

  3. Press 1 to end call

The GS3055-I should be powered up after the registration process

(Units that are not yet registered will flash top Red and Yellow LED's)
