Dip Switches Down Up

  1. = Run Mode Signal Strength Mode (shown by LED's)

  2. = DC Bell Input Siren Driver Input

  3. to 6 = LK1 Jumper on top 2 pins

Channels 3 to 6 are Normally Closed Channels 3 to 6 are Normally Open

LK1 Jumper on Bottom 2 pins

Channels 3 to 6 tripped Low to High Voltage Channels 3 to 6 tripped by Hi to Low Voltage

LK1 Jumper on Middle 2 pins (open collector)

Channels 3 to 6 tripped by taking away ground Channels 3 to 6 tripped by pulling to ground

7 = Deactivates Channel 6 Shunt Mode Activates Channel 6 Shunt Mode

(with this set Uplink to only activate if not shunted by a telephone line monitor)

8 = This switch must ALWAYS be DOWN

9 = This switch must ALWAYS be DOWN

10 = Cell Carrier uses B Channel Cell Carrier uses A Channel
