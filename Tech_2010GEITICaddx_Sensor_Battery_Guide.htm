GE/ITI/Caddx Sensor Battery Guide

GE makes 2 different door/window sensors, the left is the "Crystal" and the right is the "SAW"

The most common is the "SAW" sensor

The left cover requires pressing just above the latch then pulling cover off, the right cover requires pushing the button

The left sensor requires a 3.6 Volt Lithium battery, the right sensor requires 2 common "AAA" Alkaline

This is the most common GE motion detector

Push tab on top and pull front of motion off the backplate

Press the 2 top and 1 bottom tab and pull out the motion's "guts"

Most will have 2 "AA" Alkaline batteries (one on each side of the motion)

Some may require 1 3.6 Volt Lithium SAFT LS14500

Depending on smoke detector batteries will be 2 9-Volt Alkaline, 2 3.6-Volt Lithium or 3 3.6-Volt Lithium

Twist smoke detector from base, batteries will be on bottom, may be inside compartment

Keyfobs require removing small screw and removing cover from backplate, battery is 12V Lithium GP 23AE

Panics require removing 2 screws in back and splitting apart cover

Battery is 3.6 Volt Lithium

"Watch" or pendant panic requires removing 4 small screws in back and splitting apart cover

Slide battery from holder, battery is Duracell DL2032
