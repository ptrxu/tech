DSC WLS-914-433 Dual PIR Motion Detector

Information 4 "AA" Eveready Alkaline batteries

Mounting height: 7.5 feet

60 second warm up after powerup, LED will flash during this time

6-second delay between activation and reporting to panel (cannot be changed)

Test Mode Enter by removing motion from backplate by sliding up, test mode ends after 10 activations

Jumper J1 (Inside motion cover top left on circuit board)

This motion has no ability to slide up and down to adjust pattern

ON = High sensitive (default)

OFF = Low sensitivity
