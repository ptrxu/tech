Address Keypads (LCD only, LED keypads are always unaddressed)

(Keypads can be addressed from 1 to 16 although system only supports 1 to 8)

  1. Press 1 and F2 keys at same time

  2. Display will show "Keypad Numb = 1"

  3. Press 1 and F2 at same time to advance to next keypad address

  4. Press STATUS to exit
