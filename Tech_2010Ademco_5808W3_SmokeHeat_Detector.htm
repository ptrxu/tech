Ademco 5806W3/5808W3 Smoke/Heat Detector

Information 5806W3 = Photoelectric Smoke Detector

5808W3 = Photoelectric Smoke Detector + 135 Degree Heat Detector + 41 Degree Pre-Freeze Detector

Battery 3-volt Lithium CR123A or DL123A

During first 7 days of a low battery condition test switch is disabled

After 7 days sounder will chirp every 45 seconds, press test switch to silence for 12 hours

Loop 1 = Fire

Loop 2 = High/Low maintenance (Only supported by Vista-128FBP)

Loop 3 = Pre-freeze warning (5808W3 only, trips if temperature is below 41 degrees)

If Loop 3 is used do not learn as a zone type 8 that the installation sheet recommends!

Learn Sensor When prompted twist or remove from base to activate tamper

Test Sensor Loop 1 Fire/Smoke = Press TEST switch for 5 seconds to trigger internal sounder

All loops (1, 2 and 3)

  1. Enter panel's "RF Sniffer test" (Usually Installer Code + # + 3)

  2. Press TEST switch on smoke (All zones associated with loops 1, 2 and 3 will display)

  3. Enter Installer Code + 1 or OFF to exit

Test mode requires smoke to be mounted on base

LED Status Green LED Red LED Internal Sounder

5 second blink 1 blink every 5 seconds OFF = 20 second sensor power up

10 second blink OFF OFF = Normal

OFF 1 blink every 5 seconds OFF = Out of sensitivity (Trouble)

OFF 1 blink every 10 seconds OFF = Freeze trouble (below 41 degrees)

OFF 1 blink every second ON = Smoke alarm

OFF 1 blinks every 4 seconds ON = Heat alarm (above 135 degrees)

OFF 45 second chirp OFF = Low battery up to 7 days

OFF 45 second chirp Chirp = Low battery after 7 days
