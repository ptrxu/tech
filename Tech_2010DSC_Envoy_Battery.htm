DSC Envoy Battery

Remove top plastic screw on top of unit

Pull from top to remove panel from backplate, it should flip down

Pull panel toward you to remove from backplate pins

Remove 2 screws on back of the unit then remove pack of panel

Remove battery and disconnect by pulling wires from battery

Reconnect battery with red wire to red terminal and black wire to black terminal

Replacement battery: DSC BD 3.5-6 6-Volt 3.5AH
