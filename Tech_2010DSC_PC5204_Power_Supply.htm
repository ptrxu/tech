DSC PC5204 Power Supply

Outputs Outputs 01 to 04 can be used as PGM 03 to PGM 06 in PC5010

Wiring Wire devices to AUX (+) and Outputs (-) to O1 to O4 (provides higher current)

Program Enter 902 and wait 1 minute in PC5010 programming to enroll module

(Only 1 5204 can be supervised, additional 5204's cannot)
