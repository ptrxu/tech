DSC WS4959 5-Button Keyfob

Information 433 mhz only

2 3-Volt CR2025 Lithium button type

5 to 8 year battery life (estimate)

Range 100 feet (open air)

ESN number found on back of keyfob and inside on circuit board

Arm/Disarm buttons require 1 second keypress

Panic requires 3 second keypress

Middle button changes modes, keyfob led changes color depending on mode

5-second timeout sets mode back to Red

Red LED = Functions 1 to 4

Green LED = Functions 5 to 8

Amber LED = Functions 9 to 12
