Napco MA725/825 Basic Commands

Chip Programmable only, cannot be downloaded or programmed from keypad

Zones 725 has 4 zones, 825 has 6

Battery 12 volt rechargeable inside main control panel

Wireless No

Downloadable No

Panic Button Police Panic, press * & #

Arm Enter 4-digit code (MUST have steady STATUS light)

Disarm Enter 4-digit code

Silence Alarm 4-digit code, hold 9 for 3 seconds to clear display

Zone Status STATUS light blinks if zone open, 1 time for zone 1, 2 for zone 2 etc

Bypass Zone [S] + Zone (1, 2, 3 etc), press again to remove

Group Bypass (if enabled) Many interior zones bypass by pressing [S] twice

Alarm History Hold 2 for 3 seconds to view last alarm

Reset Hold 9 for 3 seconds

Smoke Reset Press button on outside of Main Panel

Door Chimes Hold 5 for 3 seconds

Instant Doors Hold 4 for 3 seconds before or after arming, STATUS light flashs rapidly

Phone Test Hold 6 for 3 seconds, failed test causes keypad to sound steady tone
