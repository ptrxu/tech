7. RECORD SPEECH (for Alarms sent by Private Numbers)

  1. Press SHOW/OK to enter

  2. Display changes to "House Identity" (This will identify customers house)

  3. Press and hold 2 and "Record A Message" will briefly appear then move to "Talk Now"

  4. Speak voice message (maximum 10 seconds) then release 2 key

  5. Review message by pressing 5

  6. Press NEXT, display will change to "User Term #1" (1st of 3 customized Zone Names)

  7. Press and hold 2 and "Record A Message" will briefly appear then move to "Talk Now"

  8. Speak voice message (maximum 10 seconds) then release 2 key

  9. Review message by pressing 5

  10. Press AWAY to exit

8. DIAGNOSTICS

  1. Press SHOW/OK to enter Diagnostics

  2. Display will show "Diag. Testing"

  3. Walk test and open all sensors

  4. View violated sensors along with signal strength by pressing SHOW/OK repeatedly

  5. Press OK to exit
