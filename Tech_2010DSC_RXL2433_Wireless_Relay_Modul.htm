DSC RXL2-433 Wireless Relay Module

Information Supports up to 20 keyfob buttons, 10 buttons per relay

  1. relay outputs 30VDC, 3A max

12VDC transformer

32F to 120F temperature

Compatibility WS4939 4-button

WS4949 2-button

WS4959 4-button (12 functions)

WS4969 4-button with Flashlight

WLS919-433

JP Switches JP3 and JP4 ON = Relay 1 momentary Output

JP3 OFF JP4 ON = Relay 1 Latched Output

JP3 ON JP4 OFF = Relay 1 Active for 60 seconds

JP3 OFF JP4 OFF = Relay 1 Active as long as button is pushed (WS4959 Keyfob only)

JP1 and JP2 ON = Relay 2 momentary Output

JP1 OFF JP2 ON = Relay 2 Latched Output

JP1 ON JP2 OFF = Relay 2 Active for 60 seconds

JP1 OFF JP2 OFF = Relay 2 Active as long as button is pushed (WS4959 Keyfob only)

Learn Keyfob Relay 1

  1. Remove small door on RXL2-433

  2. Press program button one time, LED should flash ON and OFF slowly

  3. Within 10 seconds press keyfob button to control relay 1 (each relay can learn up to 10 buttons)

  4. LED should flash confirming it has learned that button

Relay 2

  1. Remove small door on RXL2-433

  2. Press program button twice, LED should double flash ON and OFF slowly

  3. Within 10 seconds press keyfob button to control relay 2

  4. LED should flash confirming it has learned that button

Delete Keyfob (Deletes all keyfobs)

  1. Press and hold program button

  2. After 10 seconds LED flashs ON/OFF 5 times indicating keyfobs are deleted
