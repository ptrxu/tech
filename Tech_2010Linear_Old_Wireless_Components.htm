Linear Old Wireless Components

Found on older panels, are not supervised, only show zone open for 3 or 4 seconds

Batteries 9-volt Alkalines Door/Window, DL2032 Hearing Aid batteries for Panics

12 volt alkaline in Pendant Panics (# MN-21 or GP-23a)

Dip Switches All use 8-key dip switches to match frequency from transmitter to receiver

Motions TB1 = Pulse Count, TB2 = Walk Test (do not leave in walk test or batteries will drain)

Door Switches 2 types, Normally Closed and Normally Open contacts

(Normally open switches must be used with Normally Open receivers)

Dip Switches 1-Channel Receivers - Match Receiver and Transmitter dip switches

4-Channel Receivers - 1st 2 dip switches are channels (1 to 4), last 6 for Transmitter Frequency

Dip Switch Settings - switch 1 and 2 are which channel

  1. Off 2 Off = Channel 1

  1. On 2 Off = Channel 2

  1. Off 2 On = Channel 3

  1. On 2 On = Channel 4

RF Frequency 290-320 MHz
