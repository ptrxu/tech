Specifications 12VDC 2A max

Outputs 1 and 2 are Normally Open 1A 24VDC max

RJ 45 Network port is not used

Dip Switches S1 = Reserved (must be OFF)

S2 = Reserved (must be OFF)

S3 OFF S4 OFF = Input 1 Standard 9 to 12-volt trip

S3 OFF S4 ON = Pulse Counter

S3 ON S4 OFF = Timed Bell (Use this)

S3 ON S4 ON = Sampled Siren

Standard Input = Must be tripped by 9 to 12 VDC or an open collector

Pulse Counter = Counts number of trips from a DC voltage

Timed Bell = Must be tripped by 9 to 12 VDC, reads pulsed voltage as fire, steady as burg

Sampled Siren = Reads pulsed voltage as fire, steady as burg (Panel must have built in siren driver)
