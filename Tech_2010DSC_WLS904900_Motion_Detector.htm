DSC WLS904-900 Motion Detector

Batteries 4 Eveready "AAA" Alkaline or 2 CR123A Lithium (depends on version)

Receiver 5132 v2.1 or higher

Lockout Motion will lockout for 6 minutes if any movement seen

Test Mode Slide Motion up from mounting bracket, motion will go into walk test mode for 90 seconds

Jumpers if 2 pin jumper short to increase sensitivity

if 3 pins set to SINGLE to increase sensitivity
