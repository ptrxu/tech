ITI 60-658-95 Carbon Monoxide Sensor

This sensor is discontinued

Information 319.5mhz, sends supervisory signal every 64 minutes

Range: 500 feet open air, no farther than 100 feet recommended

Program When learning hold TEST/RESET button for 10 seconds then remove top transmitter cover

Testing 1. Wait at least 10 minutes after activation before testing

2. Make sure green power light is on

  1. Set panel to sensor test mode

  2. press and hold TEST/RESET, detector beeps, red alarm light flashes

  3. Listen for 6 to 8 interior siren beeps before releasing TEST/RESET
