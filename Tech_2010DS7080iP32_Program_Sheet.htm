  1. Example to program FRONT press 3 four times until F appears

  2. Press right arrow key to move right one space

  5. Press 6 key four times until O appears

  6. Press right arrow key to move right one space

  8. Press right arrow key to move right one space

  9. Press 8 key twice until T appears
