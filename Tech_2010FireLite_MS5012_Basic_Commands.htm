Fire-Lite MS5012 Basic Commands

This is a Fire system, powering down is usually illegal!

Zones 5 Fire Zones

Silence Alarm Press SILENCE

Reset System Press RESET (Display will show rES for 6 seconds)

(Displays active alarm or 1st previous event)

(Displays newest Alarms in memory)

(Displays previous Alarms in memory)

Zone Disable (If zone is in trouble only)

  1. Press MODE (Display goes blank)

  2. Press 3472 + ENTER/STORE (Display shows "d")

  3. Enter Zone to disable (1 to 5) (Zone will appear at far right of display)

  4. Press ENTER/STORE (Trouble LED will blink, d__ 1 to 4 displays)

Zone Re-Enable (If zone was disabled with a trouble)

  1. Press MODE (Display goes blank)

  2. Press 3622 + ENTER/STORE (Display shows flashing "E")

  3. Enter Zone to re-enable (1 to 5) (Zone will appear at far right of display)

  4. Press ENTER/STORE (Trouble LED will turn OFF)

Fire Drill 1. Press MODE (Display goes blank)

2. Press 3745 + ENTER/STORE (Display shows "dril")

3. Press RESET to exit

Lamp Test MODE + 5267 + ENTER/STORE (turns on all System LED's for 5 seconds)

Walk Test 1. Press MODE (Display goes blank)

2. Press 9255 + ENTER/STORE (Trouble light begins blinking)

3. Press 6676 + ENTER/STORE to exit

History Mode 1. Press MODE (Display goes blank)

2. Press 4478 + ENTER/STORE

Displays newest Alarms in History
