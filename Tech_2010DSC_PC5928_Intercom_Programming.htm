DSC PC5928 Intercom Programming

Information Compatible with PC5921 interior and PC5921EXT Intercom stations

Not compatible with PC5901 intercom stations

Supported PC1555MX v2.3

Panels PC1555 v3.2

PC5010 (all)

PC5015 (all)

PC5020 (all)

May not work correctly with PC1616/1832/1864

Wiring 1. Wire to control panel Keybus

  1. Wire Audio Stations RED & BLACK to COM and AUX+

  2. Wire GREEN from Station to M1 through M7 depending on station used

  3. Wire YELLOW from Station to S1 through S7 depending on station used

Volume All Speaker/Mic Adjustments are made on 5928 Board

Problems 1. Not Enrolled with 902

2. Wiring to stations backwards or incorrect

3. DO NOT DISTURB stays on = Wrong version of Intercom or incoming sounds not enabled in location 20

4. Incompatible Panel or Intercom stations

  1. Shielded wire not used between PC5928 and PC5921 Intercom stations

Feedback 1. Turn down Speaker or Mic volume

Problems 2. Re-position microphone down instead of out

3. Backs of Intercom can be cut out

Intercom (For Intercom Use, each function (except baby montior)will return to normal if 30 seconds of no activity

Instructions Initiate Page Press PAGE/ANSWER (Station will give double beep, others give rapid beep)

Cancel Page Press PAGE/ANSWER

Answer Page Press PAGE ANSWER (When page is active DO NOT DISTURB light turns on)

Ending Page Press PAGE/ANSWER again

Do Not Disturb Removes station from incoming pages (Red Light will turn On), press again to exit

Doorbell Answer When doorbell pressed all stations sound tone, press PAGE/ANSWER

Baby Monitor Hold DO NOT DISTURB 2 seconds at location to listen in (DO NOT DISTURN flashes)

Answer Phone (if enabled), press PAGE/ANSWER for 1 second

Transfer Phone Pick up house phone and press # on Phone to transfer from Intercom

Call Waiting If another call comes in press PAGE/ANSWER to toggle between calls

2-Way Voice Any location marked (2-way) is applicable for a 2-way voice installation

Program 1. Enter panel programming (* + 8 + Installer Code)

2. Enter location 802
