ITI 60-760/Caddx NX-702 2-Channel Quik Bridge Receiver Program Sheet

Specs Up to 4 sensors or keyfobs total

Example: 3 sensors can be learned into Zone 1 and 1 sensor can be learned into 2

Learn Sensor 1. Press and Hold Program button until you hear and feel a double-click

into Zone 1 2. Release the Program button

3. After 3 seconds receiver LED begins flashing

4. Trip sensor by activating tamper at arms length away from Receiver

5. After 3 seconds receiver will double-click from 1 to 4 times

(Number of double-clicks = number of sensors programmed to that zone)

Learn Sensor 1. Press and Hold Program button until you hear and feel a double-click

into Zone 2 2. Continue to hold Program button until Receiver double-clicks again

3. Release the Program Button

3. After 3 seconds receiver LED begins flashing

4. Trip sensor at arms length away from receiver

5. After 3 seconds receiver will double-click from 1 to 4 times

(Number of double-clicks = number of sensors programmed to that zone)

Delete Sensors Receiver must be defaulted

Default Receiver 1. Press and Hold Program button until Receiver double-clicks

2. Continue to hold Program button until Receiver double-clicks again

3. Continue to hold Program button until Receiver double-clicks for the 3rd time

4. Release the Program button

Learning Keyfob Any pair of buttons can be learned multiple times into the same Zone or Zones 1 and 2

Example: ARM & DISARM can be learned into Zone 1 for Maintained Arming/Disarming

LIGHT & STAR can learn into Zone 1 & 2 to control X-10 PowerFlash for Garage Door or Lights

Button Mode (What button pairs can be learned in what zone to perform what function)

Buttons Learned Performs what function Which Zone can be used

LOCK & UNLOCK Maintained Arming and Disarming Zone 1 or Zone 2

LIGHT & STAR STAR to control Lights or open Garage Zone 1 or Zone 2

LIGHT & UNLOCK LIGHT does momentary activation Zone 1

STAR does momentary activation Zone 2

(Doesn't matter which Zone this is learned into)

LOCK & STAR LOCK does momentary activation Zone 1

UNLOCK does momentary activation Zone 2

(Doesn't matter which Zone this is learned into)
