Eagle 2222 VOX Terminator

Information This is identical to the 1242 with added 1423 expander capability and jumper to enable VOX or Manual mode

Specifications 9 to 14VDC

44mA standby (with one 2172 speaker/mic)

180mA active

210mA if used as siren

Dip Switches Switch ON OFF

1 Direct Wired Trigger Auto Trip Enabled

2 Enables required for Direct/Auto Trip Enables not required

3 Trigger activates immediate Turn-On Trigger Activates 5 min. callback

4 Auto Disconnect Enabled Auto Disconnect Disabled

5 Enables Internal Siren Driver Disables Internal Siren Driver

6 2-Way active on Multiple Alarms Multiple Alarms shut down 2-Way

LED Diagnostics 1 Flash every 6 seconds = In Standby, all OK

2 Flashes every 6 seconds = 1 Ring Callback Mode waiting for Ring

3 Flashes every 6 seconds = 1 Ring Callback Mode, waiting for Acknowledgment code

4 Flashes every 6 seconds = Triggered, waiting for Station to send command

On Steady = Two Way Active

Ademco Vista panels must have *91 set to 4 if available
