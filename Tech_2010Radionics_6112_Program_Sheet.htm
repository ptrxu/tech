7.20 1Zn1 I _ I _ I _ I _ I _ I 0 to 9, B to F Enter 5 digit Zone Type, 00000 = Disabled

Typical Zone Programming

Delay Zone N/C (No Trouble or Restoral Reporting, Swinger Shunt) 24130

Delay Zone N/O (No Trouble or Restoral Reporting, Swinger Shunt) 28130

Instant Zone N/C or N/O (No Trouble or Restoral Reporting, Swinger Shunt) 20130

Interior Delay Zone N/C (No Trouble or Restoral Reporting, Swinger Shunt) 14130

Interior Delay Zone N/O (No Trouble or Restoral Reporting, Swinger Shunt) 18130

Interior Instant Zone N/O or N/C (No Trouble or Restoral Reporting, Swinger Shunt) 10130

Glassbreak Zone (No Trouble or Restoral Reporting) 20114

Window Foil Zone (No Trouble or Restoral Reporting) 20114

Fire Zone (Trouble Reporting, no Restoral Reporting) 01414

Audible Panic N/C or N/O (No Trouble or Restoral Reporting, Invisible Zone) 00111

Silent Panic N/C or N/O (No Trouble or Restoral Reporting, Invisible Zone) 00011

Independent Zone Control (If D268, D269 and D279 Expander Module is Used) 01150

Digit 1 = Zone Response Digit 3 = Zone Local Alarm Response

0 = 24Hr Zone 0 = Silent Alarm, No Alarm Output

  1. = Interior Zone 1 = Steady Alarm Output for Bells/Sirens

  2. = Perimeter Zone 2 = Aux Relay Activation for Bell Timeout

  3. = Priority 24Hr Zone 3 = Aux Relay Activation/Steady Alarm Output

  4. = Priority Interior Zone 4 = Pulsing Alarm Output for Bells/Sirens

  5. = Priority Perimeter Zone 5 = Aux Relay Activation

(Priority Zones can't be Bypaaed or Force Armed) 6 = Aux Relay Activation, Pulsing Alarm Output

Digit 2 = Zone Response to Open or Short Digit 4 = Central Station Reporting Options

0 = No Trouble, No Delay, Instant on Open or Short 0 = Non-Reporting, Local Zone Only

  1. = Trouble on Open, Instant on Open or Short 1 = Standard Reporting Zone

  2. = Trouble on Short, Instant on Open or Short 2 = Designated Zone

  3. = Trouble on Open or Short, Instant on Open or Short 3 = Swinger Shunt Zone

  4. = Delay on Open, Instant on Short, No Troubles 4 = Swinger Shunt, Designated Zone

  5. = Delay on Open, Instant on Short, Trouble on Open 5 = Standard Reporting, includes Restorals

  6. = Delay on Open, Trouble on Short, Instant on Short 6 = Restoral Reports, Designated Zone

  7. = Delay on Open, Trouble on Open/Short, instant on Short 7 = Restoral Reports, Swinger Shunt Zone

  8. = Delay on Short, Instant on Open, No Troubles 8 = Restoral Reports, Designated & Swinger

  9. = Delay on Short, Instant on Open, Trouble on Open

A = Delay on Short, Instant on Open, Trouble on Short Digit 5 = Local Annunciation Zones & Options

B = Delay on Short, Instant on Open, Trouble on Open/Short 0 = No Options

C = Delay on Open or Short, No Troubles 1 = Invisible Zone (Doesn't display on Keypad)

D = Delay on Open or Short, Trouble on Open 2 = Siren until Zone Restoral

E = Delay on Open or Short, Trouble on Short 3 = Siren until Zone Restoral, Invisible Zone

F = Delay on Open or Short, Trouble on Open/Short 4 = Sound Buzzer on Fault

5 = Sound Buzzer on Fault, Invisible Zone

6 = Sound Buzzer on Fault, Rings Sirens

7 = Sound Buzzer on Fault, Ring Bell until

Restoral, Invisible Zone
