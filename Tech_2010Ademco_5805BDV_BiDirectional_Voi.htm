Ademco 5805BDV Bi-Directional Voice Keyfob

Loop 4 must be learned to avoid CHECK 9 troubles

Information 5881 receivers require 5800TM module for bi-directional operation

Not required for Lynx or 5128RF/6150RF keypads

Check Status Press any button momentarily

House ID 1. Press OFF, ON and 1 buttons at same time, keyfob should say "House ID Mode"

2. Press ON for 10's digit and OFF for 1/s digit

3. Press 2 button to save and exit, LED should flash new house code

(If no buttons are pressed for 5 seconds keyfob will exit program mode)

Volume 1. Press OFF, ON and 2 buttons at same time until LED flashes

2. Press 1 for Low volume

Press ON for Medium volume

Press OFF for High volume

3. Press 2 to save and exit

(If no buttons are pressed for 5 seconds keyfob will exit program mode)

Record 1. Press ON, OFF and 2, keyfob will say "Message Mode"

Message 2. Press and release ON button to begin recording (up to 20 seconds)

3. Press and release 2 to save

Playback 1. Press ON, OFF and 2, keyfob will say "Message Mode - Check Message"

Message 2. Press and release OFF button to play message
