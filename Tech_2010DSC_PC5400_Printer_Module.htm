DSC PC5400 Printer Module Program Sheet

Wiring 1. Wire to PC5010 Keybus terminals

2. Connect jumper from Tamper T1 to T2

3. Connect SERIAL Printer to Com port with enclosed cord and DB-25 Jack

Program 1. Enter Panel Programming, press 902 to enable Module Supervision

2. Turn on light 1 in 801 (PC5400 Programming)
