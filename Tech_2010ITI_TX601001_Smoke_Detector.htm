ITI TX-6010-01-1 Smoke/Heat Detector

Information Compatible with GE Advent panels only

2-3.0VDC Lithium CR123A or DL123A

319.5mhz, sends supervisory signal every 64 minutes

Range: 500 feet open air, no farther than 100 feet recommended

Program Learn to panel by removing smoke from base to activate tamper

Testing 1. Press and hold TEST/SILENCE button for 4 to 20 seconds to send test signal

2. Smoke should beep and LED should turn on after 10 seconds

Cleaning 1. Place small screwdriver in slot on the cap then pry off

2. Squeeze smoke chamber where indicated then pull from base

3. Blow out or use soft brush to remove dirt

4. Line up chamber with base and snap into place

5. Line up cap and snap into base

Battery 1. twist smoke detector counter-clockwise to remove it from the base

Replacement 2. Slide battery cover away from unit

3. Observe polarity with 2-3VDC lithium battery

4. Replace battery cover then remount to base

LED 0 Flashes = No power or hardware fault

  1. Flash = Hardware fault

  2. or 3 Flashes = Unti is insensitive, smoke chamber must be cleaned

4 to 7 Flashes = Normal

8 or 9 Flashes = Unit is too sensitive, make sure smoke chamber is snapped down securely
