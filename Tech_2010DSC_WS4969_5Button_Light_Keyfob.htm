DSC WS4969 4-Button Keyfob with LED

Information 433 mhz

2 3-Volt CR2032 Lithium button type

5 to 8 year battery life (estimate)

Range 100 feet (open air)

ESN number found on back of keyfob and inside on circuit board

Middle button activates LED light

Programming Panic requires holding button for 3 seconds, to remove 3-second delay

  1. Press and hold outer 4 buttons until LED begins to flash

  2. Press AWAY

Repeat to re-enable
