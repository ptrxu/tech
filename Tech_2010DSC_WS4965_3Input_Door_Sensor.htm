DSC WS4965 3-Input Door Sensor

Information This is a 3-zone sensor Z1 can be either reed or tilt switch, select by JP3

Tilt switch angle can be adjusted and can be used on OVH doors

External sensors are Normally Closed and wiring must not exceed 25 feet

LED will flash at 10 second intervals on low battery troubles

Batteries 1 CR123A Lithium

Battery life 5 to 8 years

Programming ESN number is for Z1, Z2 is ESN number + 1 and Z3 is ESN number + 2

Example: if ESN number is 0591623 then Z2 is 591624 and Z3 is 591625
