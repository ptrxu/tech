Power Ratings 300 to 450ma total (including Aux Power, Keypads)

Keypad 25ma

Standard Motion 20ma (average)

Microwave Motion 30 to 50ma (average)

Glassbreak 25ma to 35ma (average)

Smoke Detector 40 to 50ma (average)

Enter Program Run jumper from terminals 3 and 6, hold 8 key 3 seconds

Exit Program Remove Jumper, press * and #

Move to Location Press [B] + 2 digit location

1 = scroll through digits

2 = Clears display

3 = Advances to next location

Zone 1 must be E/E

Zones 2 & 3 must be Follower

Zones 4 and 6 must be Instant

Zone 5 can be Instant Zone, Panic and/or Holdup
