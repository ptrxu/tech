Using Virtual Paper

This is very beneficial because the number of preprogrammed accounts we are currently dealing with.

These are the instructions to access the virtual paper program. Its purpose is to help us define an

unknown account number without any guesswork involvement.

  1. Click on "File" of the DICE program that you are currently using.

  2. Go to "New session" and over to "TCP/IP".

  3. Click on "TCP/IP".

  4. Enter "MONVPA" at the "HOST" space, click "OK" or press "ENTER".

  5. Login and password is vpaper, lower case. *You must enter vpaper 2 times!

  6. The operator code is VPP, in caps. * You must enter this 2 times as well!

  7. First menu screen will appear, select 1. Virtual Display.

  8. Second menu screen will appear, scroll up and select "LP Centronics 150 Mnemonics All Activity printer". Hit "Enter"

  9. Third menu will now appear, scroll to "Mark Y Marks Experimental Layout" and press "Enter"

  10. The date and time will appear, press "Enter" 2 times.

  11. At the top of the screen you will see "Turn printer on?" press "N".

  12. The screen will now start to scroll. Press and hold "Shift" and press F10 at the same time.

  13. At the lower right of your screen will now be a little box with a 3-entry menu with the "include/exclude highlighted, press "Enter".

  14. A larger box menu will appear at the lower left of the screen, scroll to " Load/Save/Delete/Clear/eXamine" and press "Enter"

  15. At the top of the screen you will now see a menu BAR, press "Enter" as the "Load" tab will be highlighted.

  16. A much larger menu will appear, press "Enter" as the "9999 account lookup" will be highlighted.

  17. Press F4, and you are now using Virtual Paper to view accounts with 9999 programmed into the account option.
