Caddx NX-488/ITI 60-873-95 SAW Glassbreak Detector

Information Range: Up to 20 feet

Batteries: 2 3-Volt Lithium DL123A (Duracell), 5 year typical battery life

319.5mhz, sends supervisory signal every 64 minutes

Testing Requires GE/Sentrol 5709C Tester

  1. Activate tester directly over glassbreak

  2. Glassbreak should go into 1-minute test mode

  3. Hold tester near glass and activate, sensor should trip

(clapping hands will only trigger status LED's not the sensor)
