ITI 60-597 HiTech Remote Keypad

Information 3 Volt Lithium DL123A or CR123A

Estimated life 5 to 8 years

Compatibility Caretaker +

Commander 2000

Concord/Concord Express

SX-V Special (Not standard SX-V)

Not compatible with Simon

Programming 1. Learn as sensor type 01 (Audible Panic) or 03 (Silent Panic)

2. Press BYPASS to learn

Keypad Sounds Press 1 and 3 at same time until confirmation beeps to turn sounds ON/OFF
