Enter Program 1. Hold 8 key for 3 seconds, keypad will beep

2. Enter 4 5 6 7 8 9, display should show dash

Program Location 1. Press S key display will show 3 dashs

2. Enter 3-digit location (use B/A for any 0)

3. Value currently programmed in location will display

  1. Scroll Value UP with 4 key, scroll Value DOWN with 7, blank location with 5

  2. When display shows desired Value press 9 then 2 to move to next location

(Or S + 3-digit location to move to elsewhere)

Move to Location 1. Press [S], display will show 3 dashs, enter 3-digit location

2. What is programmed in location will display, use 4 and 7 keys to change data

3. Once correct data displays press 2 to move to next location

Exit Program 1. Hold 8 key 3 seconds, keypad will flash and beep

2. Enter 4 5 6 7 8 9, display should return to normal

To program REPORT OPTIONS in locations 166 to 185 see below Diagram

Add location together and enter sum in location

Example: Enable Reporting for Zones 1 to 6, Keypad Panic, Fire, Duress, Fire Trle, AC Fail and Low Bat

  1. Press B/A then 1 6 6 to move to Location 166

  2. Add ZONE 1 (1) + ZONE 2 (2) + ZONE 3 (4) + ZONE 4 (8) = 15 or F, enter the F in location 166

  3. Press 2 key to move to 167

  4. Add Zone 5 (1) + Zone 6 (2) + Keypad Panic (8) = 11 (B), enter the B in location 167

  5. Press 2 key to move to 168

  6. Add FIRE ZONE (1) + AMBUSH (2) + FIRE TROUBLE (4) = 7, enter the 7 in location 168

  7. Press 2 key to move to 169

  8. Add AC FAIL (2) + LOW BAT (4) = 6, enter the 6 in location 169
