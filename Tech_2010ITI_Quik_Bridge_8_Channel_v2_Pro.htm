ITI 60-660-95 8-Channel Quik Bridge v2 Program Sheet

2 Wireless sensors per QuickBridge zone, up to 45 unsupervised Panics on zone 1

Use zones 2 to 8 for supervised Panic buttons (2 per QuickBridge zone)

Zone 7 Default RF Jam Trouble, this is disabled if sensor is learned into zone

Zone 8 Default Cover/Antenna Tamper, this is disabled if sensor is learned into zone

Power LED ON = Power OK

OFF = No Power

Blinking = Receiving RF signals (OK)

ON with Zone LED = Alarm on Zone

OFF with Yellow LED's Blinking = Receiver failed power-up test

Supervisory ON = Supervisory Zone Trouble

LED w/Zone LED OFF = Normal (OK)

Low Battery ON = Zone Low Battery

LED w/Zone LED OFF = Normal (OK)

Wiring 1. Wire + and GND to Aux + & Aux - on panel

2. Wire zones 1 to 8 to required zones on panel

3. Wire B (Battery), S (Supervisory) terminals to 2 zones for troubles (optional)

  1. Wire T (Test) to Piezo for 5VDC pulse on transmitter alarms (optional)

Learn Sensor 1. Open QuickBridge Cover

2. Press Program DIP Switch UP, Low Battery and Supervisory LED's will begin flashing

3. Press and release ADV switch until desired zone LED turns ON

4. Press and release SEL switch once, Low Battery and Supervisory LED's stop flashing

  1. Activate Tamper on sensor (if no tamper available set sensor into alarm)

  2. Zone light will blink once if sensor is learned

  3. Slide Program DIP Switch DOWN to end

Delete Sensor 1. Open QuickBridge Cover

2. Press Program DIP Switch UP, Low Battery and Supervisory LED's will begin flashing

3. Press and release ADV switch until desired zone LED turns ON

4. Press and hold SEL switch for 5 seconds, zone LED should turn OFF

5. Slide Program DIP Switch DOWN to end

N/O or N/O Zone (Default is N/C contacts)

  1. Open QuickBridge Cover

  2. Press Program DIP Switch UP, Low Battery and Supervisory LED's will begin flashing

  3. Press Tamper switch once, Battery LED should turn ON or flash

  4. Press and release ADV switch until desired zone LED turns ON

  5. Press and release SEL switch to change zone settings

LED ON = Normally Closed contact

LED OFF = Normally Open contact

  1. Slide Program DIP Switch DOWN to end

N/O or N/O 1. Open QuickBridge Cover

Trouble Output 2. Press Program DIP Switch UP, Low Battery and Supervisory LED's will begin flashing

(Default is N/C) 3. Press Tamper switch once, Battery LED should turn ON or flash

4. Press and release ADV switch until desired zone LED turns ON

5. Press and release SEL switch to change zone settings

LED ON = Normally Closed contact

LED OFF = Normally Open contact

6. Slide Program DIP Switch DOWN to end

RF Supervision 1. Open QuickBridge Cover

(Default is ON) 2. Press Program DIP Switch UP, Low Battery and Supervisory LED's will begin flashing

3. Press Tamper switch twice, Supervisory LED turns ON

4. Press and release ADV switch until desired zone LED turns ON

5. Press and release SEL switch to change zone settings

LED ON = Supervised

LED OFF = Unsupervised

6. Slide Program DIP Switch DOWN to end

Momentary or 1. Open QuickBridge Cover

Maintained 2. Press Program DIP Switch UP, Low Battery and Supervisory LED's will begin flashing

(Zns 3 - 6 only) 3. Press Tamper switch once, Battery LED should turn ON or flash

4. Press and release ADV switch until desired zone LED turns ON

5. Press and release SEL switch to change zone settings

LED ON = Momentary switch

LED OFF = Maintained switch

6. Slide Program DIP Switch DOWN to end

Keyfobs Zone 1 = Emulates Lock/Unlock buttons together

Zone 2 = Emulates Lock or Unlock Button

Zone 3 = Emulates Lights Button

Zone 4 = Emulates Star Button

Zone 5 = Emulates Lock Button (switches zone 5 output)

Zone 6 = Emulates Unlock Button (switches zone 6 output
