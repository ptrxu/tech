GE/ITI Simon 3 Battery

Open cover, use screwdriver to push latch up and open inside cover

Battery is under a cover held with 2 screws, use phillips screwdriver to remove

Remove battery from compartment, pull red and black wires from battery

Reconnect battery with red wire to red terminal and black wire to black terminal

When finished close inside cover but leave outside cover open and enter master code

Press TEST button until keypad says "Sensor Test"

Press DONE button, close cover

(This resets battery trouble)
