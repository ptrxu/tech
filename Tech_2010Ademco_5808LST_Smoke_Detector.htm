Ademco 5808LST Smoke Detector

Information Sends checkin to panel every 70 to 90 minutes

Contains an internal sounder

Embossed design on front of sensor must align with magnet design to close zone

LED Indicator 40 second flash = Normal

OFF = No power

3 second flash = In alarm

Batteries 2 3-Volt CR123 or DL123 Lithium batteries

Sensor Range 100 feet from panel (open air)

Programming Loop 1

Leave smoke on base and press TEST to learn

Lynx panels require putting panel into test mode (User Code + 5) to test smoke
