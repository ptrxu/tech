Ademco 5821 Temperature/Flood Sensor

Information Battery = Panasonic or Duracell CR123A Lithium

Can be used with Ademco 470PB flood probe (max 48 inches wire length) or T280R temp sensor

Programming Sensor has 2 internal loops and 1 loop for external probes, depending on what temperature or

flood condition is monitored the loop number must be manually changed while learning sensor.

Example: For cold temperature sensing turn both dip switches off and learn loop 1 only,

for hot and cold sensing leave both dip switches off and learn loops 1 and 2
