Ademco 6139 Alpha Keypad

Information Backlight times out after 30-45 seconds (Vista-32FB and higher panels can set backlight always on)

Should work with all panels except 4110, 4110DL, 4110XM, very old Vector, 4160 and 4180

Power Rating 40mA standby 100mA active (Backlight on, sounder on)

Address 1. Press 1 and 3 for 3 at same time within 60 seconds of powering up

2. Enter 00 to clear current address then 01 to 31 for new address
