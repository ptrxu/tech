ITI 60-832 ELM Encrypted 2-Button Keyfob

Information Works with All Concord Express panels and Simon 3 (v3.3 and above)

12 VDC Alkaline battery GP-23AE

Range: 500 ft open air

Encrypted keyfobs flash twice when activated, standard keyfobs flash once

Program 1. Press UNLOCK 3 times and hold on third press (LED should flash 3 times)

2. Press UNLOCK 2 times and hold on second press (LED should flash 2 times)

3. Hold UNLOCK button, keyfob should learn into panel (LED should flash 1 time)

(Most only have 2 buttons)
