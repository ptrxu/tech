Info Must use 5883 Wireless Receiver

Turn dip switch 6 ON for Bi-direction support

Do not set multiple keypads to the same address

Setup desired keypad address before setting up 5839

Power 1 9-Volt battery

Optional 9-Volt 110mA DC power supply Ademco part # N7703 (9-Volt center pin, 0-Volt shell)

Keypad will go into sleep mode if powered only by battery, press * to wake

Compatibility Vista-15P v1.3 and higher

Vista-20P v1.4 and higher

FA148C v1.3 and higher

FA168C v1.4 and higher
