Ademco 5800CO Carbon Monoxide Detector

Compatibility Honeywell panels that can be programmed for Zone Type 14

Information 2 3-Volt CR123 or DL123 Lithium batteries

Sends checkin to panel every 65 minutes

Range 100 feet (open air)

Carbon monoxide element lifespan is maximum 6 years

Green/Red LED Green blinks every 10 seconds = Normal

Green and Red blink every 10 seconds = Initial Power Up (lasts 30 seconds)

Green off, Red blink every second, sounder 4 beeps = Alarm or Test

Green off, Red blink every 5 seconds, sounder chirp every 45 seconds = Trouble (internal trouble)

Green off, Red blink every 10 seconds, sounder chirp every 45 seconds = Detector end of life

Green off, Red blink every 45 seconds, sounder chirp every 45 seconds = Low battery

Signals (If panel supports CO detectors)

CO2 Alarm = E162

Low Bat = E384

Trouble = E381

Tamper = E383

End of Life = E380

Programming 1. Remove from base

2. Press TEST button twice to learn (hold button several seconds each time)

3. Detector should learn as loop 1
