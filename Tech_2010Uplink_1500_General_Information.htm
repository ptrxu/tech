Uplink 1500 General Information

Information (This device uses the older AMPS network and must be replaced with a GSM backup)

  1. Wait 3 minutes before reading signal strength on initial power-up

  2. Can send SIA, Contact ID, 4/2, 3/1, Radionics Modem IIe and Radionics IIIa2 format

  3. Uplink Billing issues go to Greg Nelson at gnelson@monitronics.com

Specifications Power: 12VDC 80mA standby 1.5A while transmitting

Uplink 888-987-5465 (8AM to 8PM EST)
