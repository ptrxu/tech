Ademco 6150RF/First Alert FA260RF Keypad/Receiver

Information Keypad will operate as 5881M Receiver, max 16 transmitters

8 keyfobs can be added to keypad itself

Sensors cannot be closer than 18 inches to keypad

Supports Encrypted keyfobs (5804EN)

Supports bi-directional keyfobs, has internal 5800TM

Does not support latest 5828 wireless keypads

Can be added to older Ademco panels to provide keyfobs

Backlight Info (Version printed on side of circuit board as "SA6150RF-1" or "SA6150RF-2 etc")

V1 = Tamper button, backlight does not time out

V2 = Tamper button removed, backlight does not time out

V3 = Backlight timeout after 40 seconds

Enter Program 1. Press 1 & 3 for 5 seconds within 30 seconds of power up

2. Keypad will flash "- - 0 0" (if anything else press * to exit)

3. Enter location to program (1 to 9)

4. Enter new data then * to save and exit back to "- - 0 0"

5. Enter next location to program

Exit Program Press * until "- - 0 0" then *

Default Keypad 1. Press 1 & 3 for 5 seconds within 30 seconds of power up

2. Keypad will flash "- - 0 0" (if anything else press * to exit)

3. Press 9, keypad will flash EE

4. Press 1 to default

5. Keypad will beep 3 times display "- - 0 0", Press * to exit
