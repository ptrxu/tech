ITI 60-660-95 Quik Bridge 8 Channel v1 Program Sheet

2 Wireless sensors per QuickBridge zone, up to 45 unsupervised Panics on zone 1

Use zones 2 to 8 for supervised Panic buttons (2 per QuickBridge zone)

Power LED ON = Power OK

OFF = No Power

Blinking = Receiving RF signals (OK)

ON with Zone LED = Alarm on Zone

OFF with Yellow LED's Blinking = Receiver failed power-up test

Supervisory ON = Supervisory Zone Trouble

LED w/Zone LED OFF = Normal (OK)

Low Battery ON = Zone Low Battery

LED w/Zone LED OFF = Normal (OK)

Wiring 1. Wire + and GND to Aux + & Aux - on panel

2. Wire zones 1 to 8 to required zones on panel

3. Wire B (Battery), S (Supervisory) terminals to 2 zones for troubles (optional)

4. Wire T (Test) to Piezo for 5VDC pulse on transmitter alarms (optional)

Learn Sensor 1. Open QuickBridge Cover (2 sensors max can be learned to each zone)

2. Press Program DIP Switch UP

3. Connect wire from GND terminal (3rd from right)

4. Touch GND wire to zone 1, zone 1 LED will blink for 5 seconds

5. Activate Tamper on sensor (if no tamper available set sensor into alarm)

6. Zone light will blink once if sensor is learned

  1. Slide Program DIP Switch DOWN to end

Delete Sensor 1. Open QuickBridge Cover

2. Press Program DIP Switch UP

3. Connect wire from GND terminal (3rd from right)

4. Touch GND wire to zone to delete

5. Touch GND wire to zone to delete again, zone is now deleted

7. Slide Program DIP Switch DOWN to end

N/O or N/O 1. Open QuickBridge Cover

(Default is N/C) 2. Press Program DIP Switch UP

3. Press Tamper Switch once (Low battery LED will turn on or flash)

4. Touch GND wire to zone that will be N/O (Or all zones)

5. Zone light will turn off if N/O or turn on if N/C

5. Learn sensors with above steps

RF Supervision 1. Open QuickBridge Cover

(Default is ON) 2. Press Program DIP Switch UP

3. Press Tamper Switch until supervisory LED lights

4. Touch GND wire to zone to enable or disable supervision

Keyfobs Zone 1 = Emulates Lock/Unlock buttons together

Zone 2 = Emulates Lock or Unlock Button

Zone 3 = Emulates Lights Button

Zone 4 = Emulates Star Button

Zone 5 = Emulates Lock Button (switches zone 5 output)

Zone 6 = Emulates Unlock Button (switches zone 6 output
