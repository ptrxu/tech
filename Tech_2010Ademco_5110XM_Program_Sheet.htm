Exit Program 1. *98

Or

Power Ratings 350ma Aux output

  1. A Siren output

Keypad Address (6128/6139/6150/6160 keypads)

  1. Remove power from keypad then power back up

  2. Hold 1 & 3 for 3 seconds, "ADDR = XX" or "XX" should appear (XX = current address)

  3. Within 10 seconds enter 00 to erase old address then enter address 31

  4. Press * to save and exit

Keypad Address (6150RF keypads)

  1. Power keypad down then back up or remove backplate

  2. Within 30 seconds hold 1 & 3 for 3 seconds until flashing " - - 0 0"

  3. Press 1 and "cA XX" should appear (XX = current address)

  4. Press 31 then * to exit back to flashing " -- 0 0"

  5. Press * to exit
