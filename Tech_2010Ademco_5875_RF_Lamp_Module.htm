Ademco 5875 RF Lamp Module

Information Max load = 500W (watts)

Up to 7 keyfob buttons can be learned

(Example: 1 button on 7 keyfobs or 4 buttons on 1st keyfob and 3 buttons on 2nd keyfob etc)

Enroll Keyfobs 1. Plug lamp into lamp module, then plug module into wall

2. Press and hold button on 5875 until lamp turns ON then OFF

3. Release button, lamp should turn ON. Lamp will turn ON, continue to hold button until lamp turns OFF

4. Release button, light should turn ON

5 Press button on remote button to control lamp. if successful the light will turn OFF

If the light goes out and comes back on then the button was not learned

Delete Keyfobs 1. With lamp off press and hold button on lamp module

2. Light turnes on, then off, then on again, then off a second time

3. Release button and all remotes are deleted
