Caddx NX-481/ITI 60-807 Pet Immune SAW Motion

Information 35' x 40' coverage, mounting height 5 to 8 feet

Pet Immunity: Mount at 7 to 7.5 feet and set jumper to STANDARD

Battery: 1 "AA" SAFT 3.6 volt LS-145000 Lithium, some may have 2 "AA" Alkalines

319.5mhz, sends supervisory signal every 64 minutes

Operational temperature: 60 to 120 degrees

3-minute lockout between activations to extend battery life

SAW sensors cannot be used with Commander 2000, FonSafe, LifeGard, Caretaker + and UltraGard

Range: 500 feet open air, no farther than 100 feet recommended

LED turns on only during walk tests, not during normal operation

Learn by removing cover from back plate (push in 2 top tabs and 1 bottom tab and pull)
