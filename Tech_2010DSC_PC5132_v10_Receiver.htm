DSC PC5132/WS5132-900 v1.0 Receiver

Specifications Frequency: 900mhz

Current Draw: 120mA

Max Zones: 32

Range: 3000 feet open air

Notes This receiver does not support keyfobs or remote panics

Program 1. Enter 902 in panel programming and wait 3 minutes to enroll module to panel

2. Enter location 804

3. Enter 2-digit sub-location and enter data

(5-digit entry is required)

(Use "SN" Number from sticker on sensor)

  1. st Digit of serial number = Transmitter Type

  2. = Door/Window

  3. = Motion Detector

  4. = Smoke Detector
