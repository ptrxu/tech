Status LED's Green Rapid Blink = Successful Transmission

Rapid Blink (Yellow On/Rapid Blink) = Transmission Error, unit is retrying

Blinking Randomly = On the Air but Radio Unregistered

ON (Yellow Blinking) = Network Contact but Radio is unregistered

Yellow ON = Message Pending

Rapid Blink = Waiting for message validation after transmit

Blink once a second = Normal Operation, connected to B side carrier

Blink twice a second = Normal Operation, connected to A side carrier

Red ON = No Network Contact/RF Fault or alarm not transmitted

Periodic Blink = Loss of Communication with Panel

Rapid Blink = RF Fault/Unsuccessful transmission AND ECP Fault

Rapid Brief Blink = Transmit Error due to busy cell system, poor signal strength

or Current Limit Mode (not enough power?)

All ON = Power On or LED Test

Rapid Chaser = Power-On Reset Condition

Slow Chaser = Program Mode accessible, radio previously programmed

Slow Blink in Unison once a sec = Program Mode accessable, radio previously unprogrammed

Panel bF = Vista panel cannot communicate with 7845C or unregistered 7845C

Trouble (Below will appear on 6139/6160 alpha keypads as "Long Range Radio Fail + 4-digits listed below)

Display 0000 = 7845C has lost communication with host alarm panel

0005 = 7845C has lost contact with AlarmNet-G Network

0880 = 7845C tamper detected (cover removed)

0060 = 7845C low battery AND the control panel lost communications with 7845C

0005 = 7845C has lost contact with AlarmNet

0009 = 7845C hardware failure; requires factory service

000D = 7845C radio account is no longer active

000F = 7845C is not registered; radio account not activated

  1. = 7845C radio shutdown

0400 = 7845C Power On Reset AND the control panel lost communications with 7845C

08E0 = 7845C Tamper detected and 7845C battery low

0C80 = 7845C Power On Reset & Tamper detected

0C8F = 7845C Power On Reset & Tamper detected & Not Registered

3000 = 7845C lost DC power AND the control panel lost communications with 7845C

8000 = 7845C Battery charger failure
