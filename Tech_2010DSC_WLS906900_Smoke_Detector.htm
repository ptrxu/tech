DSC WLS906 Meridian Smoke Detector

Batteries 6 Eveready AA Alkaline

Testing Testing Smoke - Hold TEST button until Smoke sounds

Sending Signal - Use magnet next to raised line on side of Smoke until siren sounds

Trouble LED flashes every 40 to 50 seconds = Normal Operation

LED flashes and chirps every 40 to 50 seconds = Low Battery

LED flashes every 40 to 50 seconds and chirps in-between = Low Sensitivity

LED flashing every second = Smoke Detector is in Alarm

Push Button Test gives no Alarm = Low Sensitivity
