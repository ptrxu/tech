Street Smart WEL-R (Red) Installation Guide

Installation - Auto-Recognition (supported systems)

1. Unplug receiver from the wire harness

2. Wire the Red, Black, Yellow and Green to the keypad terminals of panel

RED Connect to Keypad Red or Aux (+)

BLACK Connect to Keypad Black or Aux (-)

GREEN Connect to Keypad Green

YELLOW Connect to Keypad Yellow

BROWN Connect to Zone Common (Optional)

BLUE/GREEN Connect to Zone (Optional)

BROWN/WHITE Connect to Zone if N/O required instead of BLUE/GREEN (Optional)

3. Plug harness into receiver, LED blinks 1 time on power up, after 2 seconds count flashes

Flashes Alarm Panel detected

  1. No Alarm Detected (Relay Mode)

  2. Napco 1000E/1008E/1016E

  3. Moose Z700/880/900/950

  4. Caddx 8600E/8980E

  5. Ademco Non-Addressable (4110/Vista-10/Vista-20 etc)*/First Alert

  6. Ademco Addressable Address 3 (Vista-40/50/100)*

  7. DSC PC580/1555/5010 (PC1575 works only in Relay mode)

  8. DSC 1550/2525/2550/3000

  9. Moose ZX200-210/ZX300-310/ZX400-410

  10. Moose Z1100E, Z900

  11. Nothing listed

  12. Moose Z1100

  • Ademco panels must use have 6128 keypads or higher

Press and Release Button to Arm/Disarm

Press and HOLD for 3 seconds to activate Panic

Jumpers

Panic Jumper closest to harness ON (Default)

Medical Jumper closest to harness OFF

Delay Activation Jumper farthest from harness ON (Default)

Instant Activation Jumper farthest from harness OFF

Installation - Relay Mode (non-supported systems

1. Unplug receiver from the wire harness

2. Wire the Red, Black, Yellow and Green to the keypad terminals of panel

RED Connect to Keypad Red or Aux (+)

BLACK Connect to Keypad Black or Aux (-)

BROWN Zone Common

BROWN/WHITE Zone programmed as Panic

3. Add Remote by pressing then releasing the program button on receiver

Receiver light will turn ON, press button on remote 3 times, light should turn OFF

Add Remotes

1. Press then releasing the program button on receiver

2. Receiver light will turn ON, press button on remote 3 times, light should turn OFF

Delete Remotes

1. Press and Hold program button, light will turn ON for 4 seconds then OFF then ON again
